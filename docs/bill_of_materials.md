## Notes

- The axial fan and LCD with I2C module are the most expensive individual components, together accounting for ~47% of total cost.
- The Arduino Uno can be substituted with a cheaper clone (e.g., CH340-based Uno) to reduce cost to approximately ₦3,000–₦4,000.
- For a production version, the breadboard and jumper wires would be replaced by a custom PCB, reducing long-term per-unit cost significantly.
- A single 12V DC adapter can replace the two 9V batteries for a more stable bench supply.
